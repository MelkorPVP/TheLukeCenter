Pacific Program

Flagship catalytic leadership training in Oregon.

The Pacific Program is The Luke Center's flagship training event. For over 30 years, the program has trained leaders across the public, nonprofit, and private sectors to collaborate on complex challenges.

Participants practice Catalytic Leadership through engaging sessions, peer collaboration, and applied scenarios that build lasting capacity and community.
