Have You Ever Thought of Serving on a Board of Directors? Are you passionate about helping to keep the Pacific Program fresh and relevant for today's leadership challenges?

The Luke Center Board of Directors invites alumni to share their inspiring stories and bring initiative and energy to the ongoing efforts of the Luke Center Board of Directors.

Applications are solicited each year in November to fill open terms of service. Keep The Luke Center in mind for your next civic leadership opportunity!
